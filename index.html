Welcome to the World Awerness Center

The World Awareness Center is website made by middle-schoolers who are tired of the constantly oblivious nature of humankind. Specifically, the illusion created by our minds that Climate Change is some far-off threat, a distant future, or even worse, that it does not exist. We are commited to spreading awareness about not just an issue but Climate Change, arguably the most important issue in the history of mankind.
